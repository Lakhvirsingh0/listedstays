REFUND AND POLICY

Welcome to Listed Stays—your ultimate guide to exploring the best hotels, resorts, and accommodations across Canada. Our platform is designed to simplify the process of finding the perfect place to stay, whether you’re planning a city escape, a coastal retreat, or a mountain adventure. We are passionate about showcasing Canada’s diverse travel offerings, ensuring that travelers can easily discover accommodations that meet their needs, preferences, and budgets.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal and non-personal information when you use our website or interact with us. The types of information we collect may include:

Personal Information

Personal information is information that can identify you directly. We may collect the following personal information:

  • Name
  • Email Address
  • Phone Number
  • Billing Address
  • Payment Information (when applicable)
  • Account Information (if you create an account)

Non-Personal Information

Non-personal information is data that cannot be used to identify you. This may include:

  • IP Address
  • Browser Type and Version
  • Operating System
  • Pages Visited
  • Referral Source
  • Time and Date of Visits
  • Device Information (mobile, desktop, etc.)

2. How We Collect Information

We collect information through various methods, including:

Directly from You

When you use our website, sign up for our newsletter, contact us, or make a booking, you may provide us with personal information directly.

Automatically

We collect non-personal information automatically through the use of cookies, web beacons, and other tracking technologies when you visit our website. This information helps us analyze website traffic and improve the user experience.

Third-Party Sources

We may receive information about you from third-party services that you use to interact with our website, such as social media platforms or payment providers.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for several purposes, includin

  • To Provide Our Services: To process your bookings, respond to your inquiries, and provide support.
  • To Improve Our Website and Services: To enhance the functionality of our site, tailor content to your preferences, and improve the overall user experience.
  • To Communicate with You: To send you emails, newsletters, or promotional offers (if you have opted in), and to respond to your messages.
  • To Process Payments: To complete transactions if you book accommodations through our website.
  • To Comply with Legal Obligations: To comply with any applicable laws, regulations, or legal requests.
  • For Analytics: To track and analyze website traffic and usage patterns to improve our website’s performance.

4. How We Protect Your Information

We take reasonable security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include physical, electronic, and administrative safeguards such as:

  • Secure Socket Layer (SSL) encryption for sensitive information (like payment details).
  • Firewalls and intrusion detection systems.
  • Restricted access to personal data for employees and partners who need it to perform their jobs.

However, no data transmission over the internet can be 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your information.

5. Sharing Your Information

We may share your personal information with third parties in the following situations:

Service Providers

We may share your information with trusted third-party service providers who help us operate our website, process payments, send marketing communications, or improve our services. These service providers are required to protect your information and use it only for the purposes for which it was shared.

Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information to comply with legal obligations, such as responding to subpoenas, court orders, or legal requests, or to protect our rights, property, or safety, or the rights, property, or safety of others.

Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or a portion of our business, your information may be transferred to the new owner as part of the transaction.
